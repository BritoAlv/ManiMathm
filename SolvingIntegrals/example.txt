\int \frac{1}{x^2+x+1} dx
= \int \frac{1}{(x+\frac{1}{2})^2+\frac{3}{4}}dx
u = x+\frac{1}{2}, du = dx
= \int \frac{1}{u^2+\frac{3}{4}}du
= \int \frac{1}{ \frac{3}{4} \left( (\frac{2u}{\sqrt 3})^2 + 1 \right) }du
t = \frac{2u}{\sqrt{3}}, du = \frac{\sqrt{3}dt}{2}
= \frac{4\sqrt{3}}{3*2} \int \frac{1}{t^2+1}dt
= \frac{4\sqrt{3}}{3*2} \arctan(t)
= \frac{4\sqrt{3}}{3*2} \arctan(\frac{2u}{\sqrt{3}})
